About Us...

Concepts in Tile is a small home owned buisness that has been continually producing quality custom tile installation for over 20 years. We have always been dedicated to bringing our customers the best possible service available. Since the creation of this company I have put my heart into making ever job the perfect one. I have been working in the tile industry for close to 40 years and have seen it’s ins and out. To me this job is more than just laying tile, its an artform.That is the exact attitude we put towards every job at Concepts in Tile.

The company is based out of Wappingers Falls, New York but continue to do business all across the tristate and New England areas.

Our Mission

To provide the best quality craftmanship around.
